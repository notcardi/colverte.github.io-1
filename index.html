Colverte: Make, Paint & Deliver!

Quality over quantity

We handmake our clay, sculpt and paint it to your liking.
This process takes a long time, however the quality does not disappoint.

All natural

Our clay is completely natural, and handmade from the ground.
We have 2 "grades" of clay, Brown clay (the cheaper variant), and Gray clay.
Both of our clay options are amazing, however Gray clay is a lot more stable, but harder to find.
Brown clay on the other hand is a little less stable, but a lot easier to produce.
